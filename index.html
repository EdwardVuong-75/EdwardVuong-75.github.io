CTiS: Robot for Restaurants and Fear of Unemployment

Nowadays, Automation and AI have become parts of our life. We are depending on them because of our advanced technology, they can do the jobs that we are currently working. Likewise, Automation services such as robot waiters in restaurant industry. On the other hand, this leads to the fear of unemployment to thousands or millions of people (Dodel & Mesch, 2020).
